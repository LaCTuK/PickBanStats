About

Hey. This site is about veto statistic on professional (lul) Counter-Strike: Global Offensive scene. 
 

What does all these numbers mean?

All professional (lul) CS:GO games have "veto". Some bans, some picks. Sometimes veto is not available (for example ECS and ESL not showing full veto). We just collect teams' preferences for the last three months. It could help you in predicitng veto in next matches or in betting.
